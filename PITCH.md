# CookList — 60-Second Pitch

## The problem (10 sec)
Every home cook faces the same 6pm panic: you open the fridge, feel overwhelmed, and order takeout — not because you lack food, but because no tool maps your actual day to a realistic cooking plan.

## The solution (10 sec)
CookList takes a plain-text description of your day — your schedule, your energy, what's in the fridge, who you're feeding — and returns a timed, structured cooking to-do list you can check off as you cook.

## Demo flow (30 sec)
1. Type a real description: *"Busy morning, home by 6:30, have chicken and rice, feeding 2 kids, low energy tonight"*
2. Hit **Build my cooking list** — watch the list slide in task by task, like a chef calling orders on the pass
3. Check off tasks as you cook — progress bar fills, done tasks fade out cleanly

## Why it matters (10 sec)
Millions of home cooks skip cooking not for lack of ingredients but lack of a plan. CookList turns a free-text sentence into a realistic, time-aware action list in under 10 seconds — meeting people exactly where they are, in the language they already think in.
